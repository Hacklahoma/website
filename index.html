About

Who are we?

The University of Oklahoma's Student Chapter ACM is proud to present Hacklahoma, its first inaugural hackathon! Students from all backgrounds, majors, and experience levels will come together to collaborate on projects that include mobile applications, virtual reality, and digital assistants in 24 hours. Anyone with an interest in technology comes to learn, build, and share their creations!


Why is this happening?

Hacklahoma was created to kindle creativity and innovation with the mission to improve Oklahomaâ€™s tech community. We want to make Oklahoma one of the largest places for innovation and technology and to inspire students achieve the impossible.


When? Where?

For our first hackathon, we are expecting 250 passionate students from all over the world to come together at the Oklahoma Memorial Union at the University of Oklahoma in Norman on March 3rd and 4th, 2018!

FAQ

Who can attend Hacklahoma?

Any student 18 and older can participate as a hacker!

I don't go to the University of Oklahoma though...

That's okay! Any student enrolled in a university program can attend.

But what if I'm not a student?

You are more than welcome to participate as a volunteer or mentor! If you are interested in this, contact us at team@hacklahoma.org. We will have a sign up form available soon!

Do I have to be a programmer?

No, you don't need any experience in programming to attend! Regardless of major or experience, we welcome all students who are genuinely interested in learning and technology.

Do I need a team in order to participate?

Nope! You can always go solo if that's your style, otherwise you are allowed to have up to four people at the maximum. We will have team formation sessions in the future, which we'll announce on our social media or through email!

Can I start working before Hacklahoma starts?

No, hackers are not allowed to work on their projects before Hacklahoma's official hacking period. You are, however, allowed to read up on any tools or guides out there that may help you with your project.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing a laptop, any chargers, cell phone, headphones, a pillow, toiletries, an extra set of clothes, and a water bottle. You could also bring an extension cord or additional hardware for your hack just in case.

What should I not bring?

Any weapons, drugs, and alcohol are strictly prohibited. If you're not sure about bringing something, please ask us about it in advance!

Do I have to pay to attend Hacklahoma?

Nope! Hacklahoma is free for everyone, and all meals, snacks, and refreshments are provided.

Will you be offering travel reimbursement?

Currently, we are developing a budget for travel reimbursement and will let you know more about it on our social media pages or through email!

Any other rules?

Just be respectful and have fun hacking! We also ask that you follow the MLH Code of Conduct.

I didn't find my question...

Don't worry! You can email us your inquiries at team@hacklahoma.org and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
