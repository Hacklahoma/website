What is Hacklahoma?

Hacklahoma is a hackathon soon to happen at the University of Oklahoma.

... well what is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is best described as an invention marathon. Anyone who has an interest in technology can attend a hackathon to learn, build, and share their creations over the course of a weekend in a relaxed and welcoming atomosphere. You don't have to be a programmer and you certainly don't have to be majoring in computer science. - MLH
  • Hacking as in "life hack," not the exploitation kind
  • A career fair with a twist; mentors and engineers from companies, talks, etc. (bring your resume!)
  • For beginners and pros
  • Very good real-life experience
  • Great way to network
  • Meet people from all around the nation
  • Resume builder, especially for companies like Google, Microsoft, etc.
  • Companies like GroupMe (now owned by Microsoft) started at Hackathons
